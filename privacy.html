Privacy

Privacy policy.

Last updated · April 2026

Who we are

Marketiq is a price comparison app for European markets. We are operated as an independent developer account on Google Play.

Data we collect

  • Account data — email address and username when you register.
  • Usage data — search queries, price alerts you set, country preference.
  • Device data — push notification token, only if you set a price alert.
  • Camera — used only for barcode scanning; no images are stored or transmitted.

How we use your data

  • To provide price comparison search results.
  • To send you the price drop notifications you requested.
  • To remember your language and country preferences.
  • To enforce free / Pro usage limits.

Third-party services

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, push notifications. Privacy policy.
  • eBay Partner Network — product search and affiliate links. Privacy policy.
  • Awin — affiliate product links. Privacy policy.
  • Stripe — payment processing for Pro subscriptions. Privacy policy.
  • Open Food Facts — product lookup for barcode scanning. No personal data sent.

Affiliate links

Marketiq earns a commission when you purchase through links in the app. This does not affect the price you pay. We display affiliate links from eBay, Awin, Amazon, Kelkoo, and Tradedoubler networks.

Data storage

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers located in Europe (europe-west1). We do not sell your data to third parties.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the app: Profile → Delete Account. For other requests contact us at the email below.

Age requirement

Marketiq requires users to be 16 years or older in accordance with GDPR Article 8.

Contact

For privacy questions, write to marketiqapp@gmail.com. A person replies.
