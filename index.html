01
How it works

Three quiet steps
to a better price.

Marketiq opens the twelve tabs you'd otherwise open yourself — and closes them once it has the answer.

01

Search anything

Type any product. Filter by category, condition, price range, or country. Scan a barcode if you'd rather not type.

02

Compare in seconds

Results from six eBay EU markets and the Awin affiliate network appear together, sorted lowest first.

03

Set a price alert

Not ready to buy? Name a target. We watch hourly and notify you the moment a listing dips below it.

04

Check the supermarkets

Weekly catalogues from 27 chains across Germany, France, Spain, Italy, the Netherlands and beyond.
